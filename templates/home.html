Learn languages through the songs you love

Meelange keeps lyrics, translations, and micro‑notes in sync so you can stay in the flow. Connect Spotify to unlock full tracks or use our library to start instantly.

How it works

1 — Connect Spotify or browse the built-in library.
2 — Choose your languages and pick a track.
3 — Follow the highlighted lines, jump to any lyric, and skim the grammar note that appears automatically.

Time‑synced lyrics

Every line is highlighted in real-time with a progress runner that stays locked to the track.

Side‑by‑side translation

Keep meaning visible without pausing; click either column to jump playback.

Micro‑grammar coaching

Notes appear as you move through a song so rules stick without interrupting the music.
